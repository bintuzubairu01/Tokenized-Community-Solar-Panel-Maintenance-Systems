# Tokenized Community Solar Panel Maintenance System

A decentralized system for managing community solar panel maintenance through tokenized contracts on the Stacks blockchain.

## Overview

This system consists of five interconnected smart contracts that manage different aspects of solar panel maintenance:

1. **Performance Monitoring Contract** - Tracks energy production and efficiency metrics
2. **Cleaning Coordination Contract** - Schedules panel washing and debris removal
3. **Inverter Diagnostics Contract** - Monitors electrical conversion equipment health
4. **Weather Damage Contract** - Assesses storm impact and repair requirements
5. **Warranty Tracking Contract** - Manages manufacturer guarantees and service claims

## Features

- Tokenized maintenance rewards system
- Decentralized performance tracking
- Automated scheduling for maintenance tasks
- Weather impact assessment
- Warranty claim management
- Community governance for maintenance decisions

## Contract Architecture

### Performance Monitoring Contract
- Records daily energy production data
- Calculates efficiency metrics
- Issues performance tokens for data submission
- Tracks historical performance trends

### Cleaning Coordination Contract
- Schedules regular cleaning sessions
- Coordinates community cleaning efforts
- Rewards participants with cleaning tokens
- Tracks cleaning history and effectiveness

### Inverter Diagnostics Contract
- Monitors inverter health status
- Records diagnostic data
- Alerts for maintenance needs
- Tracks repair history

### Weather Damage Contract
- Assesses weather-related damage
- Coordinates repair efforts
- Manages insurance claims
- Tracks damage history

### Warranty Tracking Contract
- Manages equipment warranties
- Tracks warranty claims
- Coordinates with manufacturers
- Maintains warranty documentation

## Token Economics

- **PERF Tokens**: Earned for submitting performance data
- **CLEAN Tokens**: Earned for participating in cleaning activities
- **DIAG Tokens**: Earned for diagnostic reporting
- **REPAIR Tokens**: Earned for damage assessment and repairs
- **WARRANTY Tokens**: Earned for warranty management

## Getting Started

1. Deploy contracts to Stacks testnet
2. Initialize system parameters
3. Register solar panel installations
4. Begin community participation
